The Cribellum is the optional-extra feature in the spider spinneret package. Located underneath the spinnerets, it has a similar appearance to the rough pad of a cats' paw. The Cribellum and Calamistrum, like violin and bow, are used together to pluck the fine silk into a 'wooly' texture. Not all spiders have a cribellum, not even all Amaurobiids have one.

An article by G.A. Milledge(read here)says that the Amaurobiid genera: Storenosoma and Oztira do not have a cribellum (ecribellate).
